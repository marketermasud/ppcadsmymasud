MY PROCESS

A battle-tested process refined across 120+ local service campaigns.

01
Free Account Audit

I start with a deep audit of your existing campaigns (or competitor analysis if starting fresh), identifying exactly where money is being wasted and where the highest-opportunity keywords are hiding.

02
Strategy & Build

I build your campaign from the ground up — tightly themed ad groups, local keyword sets, ironclad negative lists, call extensions, and a bidding strategy matched to your goals and budget.

03
Launch & Optimize

Daily monitoring after launch. Continuous A/B testing of ad copy, bid refinements by time-of-day and device, and fast elimination of underperformers to compound your results.

Weekly plain-English reports show exactly how many leads came in, what each cost, and what's next. Once the data proves what works, we scale the winners to grow your pipeline.
